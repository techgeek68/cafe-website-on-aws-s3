About Us

The Shrestha family has been delighting Kathmandu with fresh bakes and artisan coffee since 2016. At Bake&Brew, tradition meets innovation – every recipe is inspired by family heritage and made with simple, fresh ingredients.

Step into Bake&Brew and you'll be welcomed with a friendly smile and the aroma of just-baked pastries. Come for the coffee, stay for the warmth!
